What We Do

Avlixor helps brands work with the right influencers and manage everything that comes with it. Whether you want one quick shoutout or a full campaign with multiple creators, we handle the entire process from start to finish. we’ve got your back.

Our Services

Influencer Marketing

We plan and execute complete influencer campaigns. This includes selecting the right creators, handling communication, finalising deliverables, and making sure everything goes live on time. Brands don't have to deal with multiple creators or confusing messages — we manage it all in one place.

Influencer Outreach

If you already know what type of influencers you want, we take care of the entire outreach process. We contact creators, negotiate pricing, share your brief, and lock in deals faster.

What brands get:

  • Transparent pricing
  • Reliable influencers
  • Faster confirmations
  • No wasted time scrolling Instagram for creators

One-Time Collaborations

Perfect for brands that want quick results without long-term commitments. You get a single dedicated post/reel/video from a creator that fits your product.

Common uses:

  • App launch
  • Tool promotion
  • Festive offers
  • New feature announcements

Influencer Partnerships

Some brands want consistency, not one-offs. We help build long-term creator relationships where influencers post regularly and create stronger trust with the audience.

Why this works: People trust repeated visibility more than a single post.

Campaign Management

Running influencer campaigns can get messy — too many chats, deadlines, revisions, and follow-ups. We manage all of it so brands don’t have to.

We handle:

  • Briefing creators
  • Approvals & revisions
  • Scheduling posts
  • Making sure deadlines are met
  • Tracking content after it goes live

UGC Content (User-Generated Content)

Creators record natural, simple, real-looking videos that brands can use for ads, websites, landing pages, and social media.

Why brands love UGC:

  • Looks real, not scripted
  • Works better in ads
  • Builds instant trust
  • Cheaper than traditional production

We provide creators who specialise in UGC formats that perform well.

Talent Management for Creators

Creators often struggle with brand deals, pricing, negotiation, deadlines, and communication. We help them stay organised and build a strong pipeline of paid collaborations.

We support creators with:

  • Brand outreach
  • Negotiation
  • Deal structuring
  • Deadlines & approvals
  • Long-term partnerships

( When creators grow, brands also get better results — it’s a win-win. )
